About BeatScratch

BeatScratch is the insomnia-and/or-acid-induced nights and weekends musical software baby of Jon Latan√© GitHub LinkedIn Instagram, a full-stack software engineer based in the Research Triangle area of North Carolina. In real life he works on much more sensible and profitable projects for people that pay him and give him insurance.
