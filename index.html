Unleash your
musical potential

Topologica is not yet another keyboard app. Topologica turns the phone in your pocket into an instrument that can be used to play any song you could want to play, and to make it sound good.

In fact it's five instruments in one, all of which can play whatever you want on top of whatever chords you want, and each of which can be configured to sound like any one of 128 General MIDI instruments.

Our goal is to build both the keytar of this millenium and the ultimate tool for saving your musical discoveries on commutes, flights, hikes, and any other time the creative urge strikes.
